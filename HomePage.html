About Accessible Design Co.

Our Mission

Founded in 2025, Accessible Design Co. is dedicated to making the web a more inclusive place. We believe that good design is accessible design, and we work tirelessly to ensure that everyone can access and enjoy digital content.

Our Team

Our team consists of passionate designers, developers, and accessibility experts who combine their skills to create digital experiences that are beautiful, functional, and accessible to all users.

Our Services

Accessible Web Design

We create websites with WCAG 2.1 AA compliance as standard, ensuring your site works for all users.

Accessibility Audits

Our comprehensive audits identify accessibility issues and provide actionable solutions.

Training & Workshops

We teach your team how to create and maintain accessible digital products.

Consulting

Our experts provide guidance on accessibility strategy and implementation.
